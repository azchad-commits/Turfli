Our Story

Built on a Belief That the Backyard Deserves Better

Turfli was founded in Scottsdale by Karl, who spent years watching homeowners settle for substandard outdoor spaces — not because they didn't want more, but because most contractors either lacked the design vision, the installation quality, or the follow-through to deliver it.

The name comes from a simple idea: turf and living, elevated. Today Turfli handles complete outdoor living projects — from a standalone turf install to a full backyard transformation with patios, kitchens, pergolas, and turf integrated into a single cohesive design.

Every project is backed by our 15-year installation warranty and our commitment to being the contractor we'd hire for our own homes.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What does a full backyard remodel typically cost in Scottsdale?

Complete backyard remodels in Scottsdale typically range from $40,000 to $120,000+ depending on scope, size, and materials. A standalone turf install, patio, or outdoor kitchen is priced individually. We provide detailed line-item proposals after a free on-site consultation.

How long does a backyard remodel take?

Most complete backyard remodels take 2–5 weeks depending on scope. Standalone turf installs and smaller patio projects are typically completed in 1–3 days. We provide a project timeline before work begins.

Is Turfli ROC licensed and insured?

Yes. Turfli (Luxury Lawns and Greens LLC) holds an active Arizona Registrar of Contractors (ROC) license and carries full liability insurance and workman's comp. We can provide documentation on request.

What does your 15-year warranty cover?

Our 15-year warranty covers installation defects including base failure, seam separation, and drainage issues on synthetic turf projects. Product manufacturer warranties are provided in addition. Full warranty terms are included in every project contract.

Do you work in HOA communities?

Yes. We are Smartscape certified and experienced with HOA approval processes across Scottsdale and the greater Phoenix metro. We can assist with documentation and material submittals for HOA review.

Do you handle permits?

Yes. For projects that require permitting — including outdoor kitchens and certain structures — we handle the permit application process and work with the city directly. This is included in our project scope.
