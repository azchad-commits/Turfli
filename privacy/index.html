Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2025

Turfli ("we," "us," or "our") operates turfli.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or contact us for services.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

When you fill out our contact or estimate request form, we collect:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Property address
  • Project details you share in your message
  • Budget range you indicate

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information through cookies and similar technologies, including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Referring URL
  • General geographic location (city/state level)
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your estimate requests and schedule site visits
  • Communicate with you about your project
  • Send follow-up information you've requested
  • Improve our website and services
  • Understand how visitors find and use our website (analytics)
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

We use a limited number of trusted third-party services that may process your data:

  • Google Analytics: Website usage analytics. Data is anonymized and aggregated. You can opt out via Google's opt-out browser add-on.
  • Google Ads: We run Google advertising. Conversion tracking may be used to measure the effectiveness of our ads.
  • Form processing: Contact form submissions are processed and stored securely. We do not share form data with third parties beyond what is needed to operate our business.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies — small text files stored on your device — to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze website traffic via Google Analytics
  • Support advertising measurement (Google Ads)

You can control cookie settings through your browser. Disabling cookies may affect some functionality of our website.

Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions and customer communications for as long as needed to provide services and for reasonable business record-keeping purposes. We do not retain data longer than necessary.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of future marketing communications

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@turfli.com.

California Residents (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete personal information, and the right to opt-out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information. To submit a CCPA request, contact us at hello@turfli.com.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We'll note the revision date at the top of the page. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
