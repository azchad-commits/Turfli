Arcadia occupies a unique position in the Phoenix metro — neither the new-construction luxury of Scottsdale nor the urban density of central Phoenix, but something genuinely its own. Turfli designs outdoor spaces that honor that character. We treat every mature citrus tree as a design feature, frame every Camelback sightline deliberately, and select materials that feel right for the neighborhood's eclectic, quality-conscious aesthetic.

We've completed numerous Arcadia projects — from Arcadia Proper to Arcadia Lite to the Camelback Corridor — and we understand what makes each submarket distinct.

FAQs

Arcadia Outdoor Living Questions

Do you work in Arcadia Proper, Arcadia Lite, and the Camelback Corridor?

Yes — we're experienced throughout all of Arcadia's submarkets. Each has its own character and price point, and we design appropriately for each. We also serve neighboring Biltmore, Old Town Scottsdale, and Paradise Valley.

Will Turfli remove or work around my mature citrus trees?

We strongly prefer to work around mature citrus — they're one of Arcadia's most valuable and irreplaceable assets. Our design process starts by mapping every feature worth preserving. We only remove trees or plantings with your explicit direction and a clear design reason.

Do you need City of Phoenix permits for Arcadia outdoor projects?

Yes — outdoor kitchens with gas and electrical, structural shade covers, and certain other improvements require City of Phoenix permits. We handle all permitting as part of the project. We're fully licensed and insured in Arizona.
