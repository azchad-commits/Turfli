The Biltmore Area's Outdoor Living Specialists

The Biltmore Corridor Has Always Demanded the Best. Your Backyard Should Too.

The Biltmore area of Phoenix is one of the most historically significant and architecturally rich residential corridors in the entire Southwest. Frank Lloyd Wright's influence. The iconic Arizona Biltmore Resort. Decades of curated luxury real estate development.

Turfli founders Curtis Frank and Karl Baum approach Biltmore properties with the same respect this neighborhood demands. We design outdoor spaces that honor the architectural context — whether Frank Lloyd Wright-influenced, modern contemporary, or classic Southwestern — while delivering the functional luxury of today's best outdoor living.

We serve the Biltmore Estates, Biltmore Foothills, Squaw Peak Corridor, and surrounding neighborhoods.

FAQs

Biltmore Outdoor Living Questions

Do you serve Biltmore Estates, Biltmore Foothills, and the Squaw Peak area?

Yes — we serve all of the Biltmore corridor including Biltmore Estates, Biltmore Foothills, the Squaw Peak neighborhood, and surrounding areas including Arcadia, Paradise Valley, and central Scottsdale.

Can you design outdoor spaces that respect architecturally significant homes?

Yes — and this is a specialty. The Biltmore area has numerous architecturally significant properties. We approach these homes with deep respect for the original design, selecting materials and layouts that honor rather than compete with the architecture.

Do you handle City of Phoenix permits for Biltmore area projects?

Yes — we handle all permitting through the City of Phoenix for projects that require it. We're fully licensed and insured in Arizona and take full responsibility for permit compliance.
