Gainey Ranch Has Matured Into One of Scottsdale's Finest. Your Backyard Can Too.

Gainey Ranch was built around the Hyatt Regency campus and has matured into a beautifully landscaped, golf-oriented community with some of Scottsdale's most elegant homes. Properties here often need updating — and the outdoor living spaces that made sense in 1995 often need a modern refresh.

Turfli brings fresh design thinking while respecting what makes Gainey Ranch special. We design outdoor kitchens, paver patios, turf installations, and putting greens that feel right for the neighborhood's established character.

Karl Baum and Curtis Frank personally oversee Gainey Ranch projects, and we handle all HOA coordination for the community's design approval process.

FAQs

Gainey Ranch Outdoor Living FAQs

Do you handle the Gainey Ranch HOA design approval process?

Yes — we prepare full HOA submittal packages for the Gainey Ranch Community Association including 3D renderings, material specifications, and site plans. We've successfully submitted and received approval for multiple Gainey Ranch projects.

Can you modernize an older Gainey Ranch outdoor space without losing its character?

Absolutely — this is one of our specialties. We work with the existing architecture and landscape character, updating materials and layout while preserving the elements that make a property feel right for the neighborhood.

What's the most popular outdoor living upgrade in Gainey Ranch?

Paver patio replacements (updating old aggregate or tile), outdoor kitchen additions, and artificial turf installations are the most common. Many Gainey Ranch homeowners also add pergolas to update the look while improving shade coverage.
