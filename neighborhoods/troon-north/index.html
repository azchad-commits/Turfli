FAQs

Troon North Outdoor Living Questions

Do you handle Troon North HOA architectural review submissions?

Yes — we're experienced with the Troon North community HOA process. We prepare full review packages with 3D renderings, material specs, and site plans. We know what's required and have successfully navigated the process for multiple Troon North projects.

Can you design a putting green that complements the Pinnacle/Monument course aesthetic?

Yes — and many Troon North homeowners specifically request this. We use natural-looking undulation, desert fescue fringe areas, and native-adjacent planting borders to create putting greens that feel organically connected to the North Scottsdale desert environment.

Do you work with golf view lots in Troon North?

Yes — golf course view lots are among our favorite Troon North projects. We design to maximize and frame those views rather than block them. Structure heights, orientation, and material colors are all chosen to complement the golf course backdrop.
