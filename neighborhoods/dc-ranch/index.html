FAQs

DC Ranch Outdoor Living Questions

Do you handle DC Ranch HOA design review submissions?

Yes — we're experienced with the DC Ranch Community Association's design review process. We prepare full submittal packages including 3D renderings, material samples, and specification sheets. We've been through the process multiple times and know what's required for approval.

What outdoor living elements are most popular in DC Ranch?

Putting greens, outdoor kitchens, travertine or natural stone patios, and motorized louvered pergolas are the most requested upgrades. Many DC Ranch homeowners also add landscape lighting to extend outdoor living into the evenings.

Can you design to preserve the McDowell Mountain views?

Absolutely — view preservation is a core part of our design philosophy for DC Ranch. We map sightlines in 3D and design every structural element to maximize views rather than obstruct them.

How long does a DC Ranch project typically take from design to completion?

Most DC Ranch projects take 4–8 weeks total including design, HOA approval, and construction. We can often accelerate the construction phase for clients with firm timelines.
