Scottsdale Outdoor Kitchen FAQs

Common Questions

Do outdoor kitchens require permits in Scottsdale?

Yes — outdoor kitchens with gas lines and electrical connections require permits from the City of Scottsdale. Turfli handles all permitting as part of the project. This is important for home insurance, resale, and safety. We won't build a kitchen that skips permits.

How long does an outdoor kitchen build take in Scottsdale?

Most Scottsdale outdoor kitchens take 3–5 weeks from permit approval to completion. The design and permit phase typically takes 2–4 weeks beforehand. Total project timeline is typically 6–9 weeks from consultation to final walkthrough.

What grill brands do you work with?

We work with all major outdoor cooking brands including Weber, Lion, Bull, Blaze, and Thor Kitchen. For estate-level projects, we also work with Fire Magic, DCS, and Viking outdoor appliances. We recommend based on budget and use case.

Can you add an outdoor kitchen to an existing patio?

Yes — we regularly retrofit outdoor kitchens onto existing patios in Scottsdale. We'll assess the space, design around existing features, and extend the patio if needed for proper clearance and layout.
