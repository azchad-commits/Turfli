Common Questions

Scottsdale Outdoor Living FAQ

Does Turfli work in gated communities and HOA neighborhoods in Scottsdale?

Yes — HOA and gated community work is a significant part of our business. We work regularly in Silverleaf, DC Ranch, Desert Mountain, Troon North, Gainey Ranch, McCormick Ranch, and most other gated communities throughout Scottsdale. We prepare and submit architectural review packages and know how to navigate each community's specific standards.

Are your turf products Smartscape certified for Scottsdale HOAs?

Yes. All turf products we install are Maricopa County Smartscape certified, which satisfies the requirements of virtually all Scottsdale-area HOAs that regulate landscape materials. We can provide certification documentation as part of any project.

How soon can Turfli start a project in Scottsdale?

For straightforward turf and paver projects, we can often schedule within 2–4 weeks. For larger design-build projects requiring 3D renderings and permits, design completion takes 1–3 weeks and permit approval typically adds 2–6 weeks depending on the jurisdiction. We'll give you accurate scheduling at your free consultation.

What does a typical backyard remodel cost in Scottsdale?

Backyards in Scottsdale vary enormously in scope. Simple turf and paver projects start around $8,000–$20,000. Full backyard transformations with multiple elements typically run $50,000–$150,000. Luxury estate projects in Silverleaf, DC Ranch, and similar communities often reach $200,000–$400,000+. We work across all ranges and design to your budget from the first conversation.
