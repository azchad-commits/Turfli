Scottsdale Landscape Design FAQs

Common Questions

How much does landscape design cost in Scottsdale?

Turfli's design process is included with your project — there's no separate design fee. We provide the full 3D design, site plan, and itemized quote as part of the free consultation. Construction costs vary by scope: full backyard transformations typically range from $30,000–$150,000+ depending on size and services.

How long does the design process take before construction starts?

Typically 1–2 weeks for the initial 3D design after the site consultation. We revise based on your feedback until you're ready to approve. For HOA communities, add 2–4 weeks for the approval process.

Do you design for Scottsdale's specific climate and soil conditions?

Yes — all of our designs account for Arizona's extreme heat, caliche soil, drainage requirements, and monsoon seasons. We use materials and construction methods that are specifically rated for Scottsdale conditions.

Can you incorporate pool landscaping and surrounds into the design?

Yes — pool surrounds, water features, and outdoor living spaces are among our most common Scottsdale design combinations. We coordinate with pool contractors if needed.
