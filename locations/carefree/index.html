Common Questions

Carefree Outdoor Living FAQs

Do you handle the full design and construction of Carefree estate outdoor projects?

Yes — Turfli is a true design-build contractor. We handle site assessment, 3D design, permitting, construction, and final walkthrough entirely in-house. For Carefree properties, this single-team approach is especially valuable given the complexity of most projects.

Can you design around existing desert features like saguaros and boulders?

Absolutely — and we strongly recommend it. Carefree's natural features are irreplaceable. Our design process starts with a thorough site assessment that maps every feature worth preserving. We design the outdoor space around them, not through them.

What's involved in a Carefree estate outdoor project?

Most Carefree projects are multi-zone and complex: pool surrounds, outdoor rooms, shade structures, kitchens, putting greens, and lighting. We typically spend 2–4 weeks on the design phase before any construction begins, ensuring every detail is right.

Do you serve the Boulders area and other Carefree resort communities?

Yes — we serve all of Carefree including properties near The Boulders, as well as neighboring Cave Creek and North Scottsdale's luxury communities.
