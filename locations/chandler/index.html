FAQs

Chandler Outdoor Living Questions

Do you serve all parts of Chandler including Fulton Ranch, Ocotillo, and Sun Groves?

Yes — we serve all of Chandler including Fulton Ranch, Ocotillo, Sun Groves, Arden Park, Dobson Ranch, and all surrounding neighborhoods. We also serve Gilbert, Tempe, Mesa, and the broader SE Valley.

How does artificial turf hold up with kids and pets in Chandler?

Extremely well. We use 50–80 oz face weight turf with polyurethane backing for tear resistance and ZeoFill infill for pet turf areas. The products we install are specifically designed for high-use family environments and hold up for 15–20 years.

Do you need permits for outdoor kitchens in Chandler?

Yes — outdoor kitchens with gas lines and electrical require permits from the City of Chandler. We handle all permitting and inspections as part of the project. This is important for resale value and home insurance.

What's the typical cost of a full backyard remodel in Chandler?

Scope varies widely. Turf-only starts around $8–12/sq ft installed. Full backyard projects (turf + pavers + pergola) typically run $25,000–$80,000+. Contact us for a free on-site consultation and exact quote.
