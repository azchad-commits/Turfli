Frequently Asked Questions

Cave Creek Outdoor Living FAQs

Do you work on equestrian properties in Cave Creek?

Yes — we regularly work on equestrian properties with large acreage. We use turf and hardscape products rated for heavy use, and we design around existing equestrian facilities. We understand the unique needs of Cave Creek estate properties.

Can you handle large, multi-zone outdoor projects?

Absolutely. Many Cave Creek projects involve multiple zones — putting greens, pool surrounds, covered outdoor kitchens, fire pit areas, and more. We 3D design the full site plan before construction begins so you see exactly how everything connects.

Do you work in Carefree as well as Cave Creek?

Yes — we serve both Cave Creek and Carefree, as well as Scottsdale's northern areas including Desert Mountain, Troon North, and surrounding communities.

How do you handle the monsoon season with shade structures?

All our pergolas and shade structures are engineered for Arizona wind loads including monsoon gusts. We use heavy-gauge aluminum framing, deep footings, and hardware rated for desert conditions. Motorized louvered roofs automatically close when wind is detected.
