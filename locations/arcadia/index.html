FAQs

Arcadia Outdoor Living Questions

Do you serve Arcadia Proper, Arcadia Lite, and the Camelback Corridor?

Yes — we serve all Arcadia submarkets including Arcadia Proper, Arcadia Lite, the Camelback Corridor, and surrounding areas of Phoenix near the 44th St corridor. We're also experienced in neighboring Biltmore and Gainey Ranch properties.

How do you handle mature trees and existing landscaping in Arcadia yards?

We treat existing character elements as design features. Mature citrus trees, native plants, and established landscaping get mapped into the design as focal points. We build around them, not over them — and always consult with you before removing anything.

Can you match the architectural style of Arcadia's historic ranch homes?

Yes — we're experienced designing for Arcadia's diverse architecture, from original 1950s-60s ranch homes to new construction modern builds. Materials and structure styles are chosen to complement each property's character.

Do you need permits for outdoor projects in Phoenix/Arcadia?

Yes — outdoor kitchens with gas and electrical, structural shade covers, and certain other improvements require City of Phoenix permits. Turfli handles all permitting as part of the project. We're fully licensed and insured in Arizona.
