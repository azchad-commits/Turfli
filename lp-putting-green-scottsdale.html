Common Questions

Putting Green FAQ

How much does a backyard putting green cost in Scottsdale?

Most residential putting greens in Scottsdale range from $8,000 to $40,000+ depending on size, number of holes, add-ons like sand traps or lighting, and surround materials. A starter 2-hole green (150–200 sq ft) typically starts around $8,000–$12,000. A full practice complex with chipping area and lighting runs $20,000–$40,000+. We provide free itemized quotes with no surprises.

How realistic does the ball roll feel compared to a real putting green?

Our Tigerturf putting surfaces are engineered specifically for consistent ball roll and pace. The infill compound and fiber construction mimic closely mowed bent grass. Virtually all clients say the roll feels surprisingly authentic, especially after we dial in the nap direction and infill density to match your preferred speed.

Can you build the green with breaks, slopes, and elevation changes?

Yes — that's exactly what separates our greens from a flat carpet install. We excavate and regrade to create custom elevation changes, then shape the base to produce the breaks you want. You can request subtle borrows, severe slopes, or anything in between. We walk the design with you before breaking ground so you approve every contour.

How long does installation take?

Most residential putting greens take 1–3 days to install. A compact 2-hole green can often be completed in a single full day. Larger estate complexes with significant excavation, lighting, and hardscape elements may take 3–5 days. We'll give you an accurate timeline with your quote so you can plan accordingly.

Does the synthetic turf hold up in Arizona summer heat?

Yes. All turf products we install are rated for extreme UV exposure and Arizona heat. The fiber won't fade, degrade, or lose its shape. Surface temperatures are higher than natural grass on hot days — we recommend morning or evening sessions in peak summer, or adding a shade structure, which we can also build. The turf itself performs year-round without irrigation or maintenance.

Can you add lighting so I can putt at night?

Absolutely. We integrate landscape lighting into most Luxury and Estate packages. We can also add lighting to any green as an upgrade. Low-voltage LED fixtures along the fringe create a beautiful atmosphere and let you practice after Arizona's summer sun sets. We design the lighting plan to avoid glare on the putting line.
