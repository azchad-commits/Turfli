Our Story

Built on a Belief That the Backyard Deserves Better

Turfli was founded in Scottsdale by Karl Baum and Curtis Frank, who spent years watching homeowners settle for substandard outdoor spaces — not because they didn't want more, but because most contractors either lacked the design vision, the installation quality, or the follow-through to deliver it.

The idea was simple: treat every backyard project with the same care you'd give a luxury interior remodel. That means starting with design, using premium materials, managing the full scope, and backing the work with a real warranty.

Today Turfli handles complete outdoor living projects across Scottsdale, Paradise Valley, and the Phoenix metro — from a standalone turf install to a full backyard transformation with patios, kitchens, pergolas, putting greens, and turf integrated into a single cohesive design.

Every project is backed by our 15-year installation warranty and the commitment to be the contractor we'd hire for our own homes.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Pricing & Project Scope

What does a full backyard remodel cost in Scottsdale?

Complete backyard remodels in Scottsdale typically range from $40,000 to $120,000+ depending on scope, size, and materials. Standalone projects like artificial turf, patios, or outdoor kitchens are priced individually. We provide detailed line-item proposals after a free on-site consultation.

How much does artificial turf installation cost in Phoenix?

Artificial turf installation in the Phoenix and Scottsdale area typically runs $12–$22 per square foot installed, depending on turf grade, base preparation requirements, drainage complexity, and project size. We provide detailed written quotes after a free site visit.

How long does a backyard remodel take?

Most complete backyard remodels take 2–5 weeks depending on scope. Standalone turf installs and smaller patio projects are typically completed in 1–3 days. We provide a detailed project timeline before work begins and keep you updated throughout.

Do you offer financing?

Yes. We work with financing partners to make luxury backyard projects accessible. Financing options are available for qualified homeowners. Ask about current financing programs during your free design consultation.

Licensing, Warranty & HOA

Is Turfli ROC licensed and insured?

Yes. Turfli (Luxury Lawns and Greens LLC) holds an active Arizona Registrar of Contractors (ROC) license and carries full general liability insurance and worker's compensation. We provide documentation on request prior to any project start.

What does your 15-year warranty cover?

Our 15-year installation warranty covers defects in workmanship including base failure, seam separation, drainage issues, and structural settling on all synthetic turf and hardscape projects. Product manufacturer warranties are provided in addition. Full warranty terms are included in every project contract.

Do you work in HOA communities?

Yes. We are Smartscape certified and experienced with HOA approval processes across Scottsdale, Paradise Valley, DC Ranch, Gainey Ranch, McCormick Ranch, and other master-planned communities. We assist with material submittals, documentation, and the full HOA review process.

Do you handle permits?

Yes. For projects requiring permits — including outdoor kitchens, certain structures, and electrical work — we handle the permit application process and coordinate with the city directly. This is included in our project scope and proposal.
