Common Questions

Artificial Turf FAQ

How much does artificial turf installation cost in Scottsdale?

Premium artificial turf installation in Scottsdale typically runs $12–$22 per square foot installed, depending on the turf product, site preparation required, and total area. A 500 sq ft backyard lawn averages $6,000–$10,000. Pricing includes demo, base prep, turf, infill, and cleanup. We provide free detailed quotes with no surprises.

How long does artificial turf last in Arizona?

Quality artificial turf properly installed in Arizona typically lasts 15–25 years. The turf fiber is UV-stabilized to resist Arizona's extreme sun. Proper base installation is the most important longevity factor — a well-built base prevents the settling and drainage issues that shorten turf life.

Does artificial turf get too hot in Arizona summers?

Synthetic turf does absorb heat and can reach surface temperatures of 150–170°F on a 110°F day in direct sun. We recommend turf with cooling infill technology, shaded placement where possible, or simply hosing down the turf before use on the hottest days. Most homeowners find this a reasonable tradeoff for zero water and maintenance.

Is artificial turf safe for kids and pets?

Yes — the premium turf products we install are non-toxic and safe for children and pets. For pet-specific areas, we recommend antimicrobial or ZeoFill infill to neutralize odors and inhibit bacterial growth. We install pet-grade drainage systems that allow liquids to pass through quickly.

Does artificial turf require maintenance?

Minimal. Occasional rinsing to remove dust and debris, periodic brushing to keep fibers upright, and removing solid pet waste. No watering, no mowing, no fertilizing. We recommend an annual professional brushing and infill top-off to maintain appearance and performance.
