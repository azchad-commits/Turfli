Questions

Landscape Design FAQ

Is there a separate charge for the design?

The initial consultation and conceptual sketch are always free. Full 3D renderings and detailed design work are included in the build contract — no separate design fee when you proceed with construction. If you want standalone design-only services, we can discuss that separately.

What areas of Scottsdale do you serve?

We serve all of Scottsdale, Paradise Valley, North Scottsdale, Arcadia, Cave Creek, Fountain Hills, Chandler, Gilbert, Tempe, Mesa, Peoria, and the greater Phoenix Metro. We regularly work in Silverleaf, DC Ranch, Desert Mountain, Troon North, Gainey Ranch, and other premier gated communities.

How long does the design phase take?

Initial concepts are typically ready within 1–2 weeks of the site visit. More complex estates may require 3–4 weeks to develop full 3D renderings and a detailed proposal. We'll give you a specific timeline at your consultation.

What if I only want one element — not a full remodel?

No problem. We do focused scopes all the time — just turf, just a patio, just a putting green. We apply the same design-build approach to any project size. The design consultation is always free regardless of scope.
