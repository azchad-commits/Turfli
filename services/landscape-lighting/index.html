Questions

Landscape Lighting FAQ

How much does landscape lighting cost in Scottsdale?

Landscape lighting systems in Scottsdale typically range from $2,500 for a basic path and uplight package to $15,000+ for comprehensive systems with smart controls covering a large estate. Most residential installations fall between $4,000 and $8,000. We provide free design consultations and itemized quotes.

Is LED landscape lighting worth it over standard halogen?

Yes — modern LED landscape fixtures use 75–80% less energy than comparable halogen fixtures, produce virtually no heat (important in Arizona), last 10–25x longer, and are available in the same warm color temperatures that made halogen popular. The payback period on the energy savings is typically 2–3 years.

Can landscape lighting be added to an existing outdoor space?

Absolutely. We regularly retrofit lighting into completed backyards — adding uplights for palms, path lights along existing walkways, and kitchen task lighting over existing islands. The work is minimally invasive and can usually be completed in 1–2 days without disturbing surrounding landscaping or hardscape.

Is landscape lighting included in full backyard remodel projects?

For Luxury and Estate-level backyard remodels, landscape lighting is typically included as part of the complete scope. For mid-range projects, it's available as an add-on. We always flag lighting design in our proposals so you can include or phase it based on budget.
