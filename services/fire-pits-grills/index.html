Common Questions

Fire Pit FAQ

How much does a gas fire pit cost in Scottsdale?

Custom gas fire pits in Scottsdale typically range from $3,500 for a simple concrete insert to $15,000+ for a large masonry fire pit with stone surround, gas line, and integrated seating wall. Most projects fall in the $5,000–$10,000 range. We provide free itemized quotes.

Do fire pits require permits in Scottsdale?

Yes — any gas fire feature with a permanent gas line connection requires a permit in Scottsdale, Paradise Valley, and most Phoenix-area municipalities. We handle the full permit process, including gas line inspection and final fire department sign-off, as part of every build.

What fuel source works best for Arizona fire features?

Natural gas is the most popular option in Scottsdale — no tanks to refill, consistent flame, and clean burn. Propane works well in areas without natural gas service. Wood-burning fire pits are an option but require more maintenance and may be subject to burn restrictions during high-fire weather.

Can a fire pit be added to an existing patio?

Yes. We routinely add fire features to existing paver patios, concrete slabs, and travertine decks. We core drill for gas lines where needed and design the fire feature to integrate seamlessly with your existing patio surface and landscaping.
