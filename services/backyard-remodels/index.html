Common Questions

Backyard Remodel FAQ

How much does a full backyard remodel cost in Scottsdale?

Full backyard remodels in Scottsdale range from $40,000 to $300,000+ depending on scope. A mid-range transformation with turf, travertine patio, outdoor kitchen, and landscaping typically runs $60,000–$120,000. Luxury estates with custom putting greens, full outdoor kitchens, pergolas, and extensive hardscape often reach $150,000–$300,000. We work across all budget ranges and design to your budget, not beyond it.

How long does a backyard remodel take?

Timeline depends on scope. Focused projects — patio plus turf — can be completed in 1–2 weeks. Full backyard transformations with multiple trades and permits typically take 4–12 weeks from permit approval. We give you a detailed project schedule with milestones so you always know what's happening and when.

Do you handle permits?

Yes — permit pulling is included for all work requiring it. This includes outdoor kitchens with gas and electrical, structural shade structures, and grading/drainage projects. We submit drawings, track approvals, schedule inspections, and get final sign-off. You don't manage any of this.

Do you work in gated communities and HOA neighborhoods?

Regularly. We have extensive experience in Silverleaf, DC Ranch, Desert Mountain, Troon North, Gainey Ranch, and most gated communities throughout Scottsdale and Paradise Valley. We handle HOA architectural submittals and understand the approval processes in these communities.

Is the 3D design included?

The initial consultation and conceptual sketch are always free. Full 3D renderings are included as part of the build contract — there's no separate design fee when we proceed with construction. This is one of the advantages of working with a design-build contractor: the design investment is tied to the project outcome, not an upfront fee.
