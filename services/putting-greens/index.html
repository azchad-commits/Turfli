Questions

Putting Green FAQ

How much does a backyard putting green cost in Scottsdale?

Most residential putting greens range from $8,000 to $40,000+ depending on size, number of holes, and add-ons like sand traps and lighting. A 2-hole starter green (150–200 sq ft) typically starts around $8,000–$12,000. An estate practice complex with lighting and chipping areas runs $25,000–$40,000+.

Can you build real breaks and slopes into the green?

Yes — that's the core of what sets our greens apart. We excavate, regrade, and shape the base to produce the exact breaks and undulations you want. You walk through the design with us before we break ground so you approve every contour.

Does synthetic turf hold up as a putting surface in Arizona heat?

Yes. The Tigerturf surfaces we install are specifically engineered for outdoor putting in UV and extreme-heat environments. Fiber performance, ball roll consistency, and surface texture are maintained year-round. Surface temperatures on hot days are higher than natural grass — morning or evening use, or adding shade, is recommended in peak summer.

Can you add lighting for evening putting?

Absolutely. We integrate low-voltage LED landscape lighting into most Luxury and Estate packages. Lighting can also be added as an upgrade to any green. We design the fixtures to illuminate the green without creating glare on the putting line.
