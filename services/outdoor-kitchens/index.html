Common Questions

Outdoor Kitchen FAQ

How much does a custom outdoor kitchen cost in Scottsdale?

Custom outdoor kitchens in Scottsdale typically range from $15,000 for a simple BBQ island to $80,000+ for a full kitchen with pergola, pizza oven, bar, and premium appliances. A full-featured island with grill, side burners, refrigerator, and granite countertop typically runs $20,000–$40,000. We provide free itemized quotes.

Do outdoor kitchens in Scottsdale need permits?

Any outdoor structure with gas, electrical, or plumbing connections requires permits in Scottsdale and most Phoenix-area municipalities. We handle the full permit process as part of every build, including design drawings, submittals, inspections, and final sign-off.

What countertop materials hold up best outdoors in Arizona?

Granite and porcelain tile are the most heat-resistant and durable options for outdoor kitchen countertops in Arizona. We specifically avoid materials that warp, fade, or degrade in UV exposure and extreme heat. We'll walk through material options during your consultation.

How long does an outdoor kitchen build take?

Standard outdoor kitchen builds take 2–4 weeks from permit approval to completion. Simpler BBQ island projects can be done in 1–2 weeks. Complex projects with extensive masonry, multiple trades, and permitting may take 4–6 weeks. We'll give you a detailed timeline with your quote.
