Common Questions

Desert Landscaping FAQ

How much does desert landscaping cost in Scottsdale?

Desert landscaping projects in Scottsdale typically range from $5,000 for a simple front yard with DG, plants, and drip irrigation to $40,000+ for a full backyard redesign with boulders, mature specimen plants, lighting, and irrigation. We provide free itemized quotes based on your specific yard and goals.

Do you work in HOA communities?

Yes — we're experienced working within HOA guidelines throughout Scottsdale and Paradise Valley, including Silverleaf, DC Ranch, Troon North, Gainey Ranch, McCormick Ranch, and Desert Mountain. We can prepare HOA submittal packages and coordinate approvals as part of your project.

What's the difference between Smartscape and regular landscaping?

Smartscape is a water-conservation program from the Arizona Department of Water Resources that certifies landscapes using drought-tolerant plants and efficient irrigation. Smartscape-compliant landscapes typically use 50–70% less water than traditional landscaping. Turfli is certified to design and install Smartscape-compliant landscapes.

How long does a desert landscape project take?

Most residential desert landscaping projects take 3–10 days from start to finish, depending on scope. Front yard refreshes can be done in 1–2 days. Full backyard designs with boulders, irrigation, and mature plants may take 2–3 weeks including HOA approval time.
